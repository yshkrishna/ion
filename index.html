Who We Are

IonRobo is not a traditional software company. We exist to solve the hardest problems in autonomous systems — building adaptive autonomy that perceives, reasons, and acts in real-world environments without human hand-holding.
What We Build

The intelligence layer
for machines that act.

We build the core AI powering machines that move, decide, and adapt — entirely on their own.

🤖

Autonomous Robots

Robots that navigate unpredictable environments — factories, farms, warehouses, cities. Our systems perceive, plan, and execute in real time. No remote operator required.

🧠

Distributed AI Agents

AI agents that coordinate like a hive mind — self-healing, self-optimising, and secure across thousands of nodes. When one fails, the swarm adapts. Instantly.

⚡

Edge-Native Software

Software that runs on tiny devices, drones, and industrial machines with millisecond decisions. No cloud round-trip. No latency bottleneck. Pure autonomous speed.
